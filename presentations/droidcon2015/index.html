Higher-Level Abstractions are Universal

Regardless of what engine or framework or engine you use, there are a core set of ideas you'll work with.

Scene

This is the world

Objects have a 3D position and rotation

Traditionally, x is left to right, y is down to up, and z is back to front.
(0,0,0) is dead center.

Objects have a Hierarchy

They can have parent-child relationships.
Parent's positioning affects children's.
The scene is the ultimate parent.

Camera

We render (draw) what we can see through a camera.

Let there be Light

Without light we cannot see anything!

Visible Objects are Made of Meshes and Materials

Meshes define the shape.
Materials define the skin, and are made up of textures (images).

Scripting says what happens on each frame

Cast a Ray

A ray is a line that starts in the camera position and goes inifinitely in the direction the camera is facing. Anything intersecting the ray is in line of sight.

Simulator Sickness is Real

Need to hit 60-90 fps. Motion data already laggy. More graphics you push the harder this gets.

Also ...

Don't move the user or not respond to their head movement.
If you do move them, use constant velocity.
